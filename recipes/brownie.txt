- 110g butter melted
- 220g sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 35g cocoa powder
- 65g plain flour
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder


## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees.
1. Spray an 8x8 inch baking pan with baking spray.
1. Whisk together melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract.
1. Whisk in the cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder.
1. Spread batter evenly into greased baking pan.
1. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes.
